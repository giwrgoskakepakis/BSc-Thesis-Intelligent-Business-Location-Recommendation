In this folder I perform the ensembling of the 3 approaches to acquire the final top3 file.


- Comparison: I created some stats/visualizations to compare the 3 files
- Combination: i used the Ensemble method to combine the results from all 3 approaches, the weights for each approach were calculated based on the 
  percentage of central suggestions each one had, since we pre-decide this is important

The final file is 'final_top3.csv'
