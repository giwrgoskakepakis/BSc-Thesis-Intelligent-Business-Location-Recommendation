As it happened with the ELSTAT files for demographic data, they are categorized in 3 categories: 'A', 'B', 'C'. From what i understand,
- Category 'A' refers to the whole Regional Unit of Magnesia --> the data is not useful for my project. 
- Category 'B' refers to the Municipalities of Magnesia --> could be useful, but more detailed information may be better
- Category 'C' refers to the Municipal Communities of Magnesia --> these are the smaller units so probably the best

I think 'Γ04' is the best at giving me the mapping i need, like in the demographic data. I used this file to make the mapping from Municipal Communities
