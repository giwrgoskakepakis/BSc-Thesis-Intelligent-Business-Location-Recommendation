This folder contains shapefiles about different scales/locations of my project. I currently have for: 

- Shapefiles for Greece: obtained by ELSTAT
- Shapefiles for Magnesia: filtered by the previous shapefile just the polygons that belong to Magnesia
- Shapefiles for Volos: filtered by the previous shapefile just the polygons that belong to the Municipality of Volos
- Shapefiles for Grids: grid polygons across all of Magnesia (NOT USED)



Also, I have some shapefiles extracted by QGIS that help me create new features to the candidates by measuring their distance
from various POIs:

- Shapefiles for Main Road: contain the main roads in Magnesia
- Shapefiles for Transport Stops: contain the transport stops in Magnesia
- Shapefiles for University: contain the university locations in Magnesia
