This folder contains the Information for the step-wise UPDATING of the business entries dataset, as we enrich it with new features each time:

- 1. Joining - Business Data - NACE: adding the features 'NACE Code', 'NACE Description (EN)' to the business entries
- 2. Joining - Business - Economic-Demographic Data: adding the feature 'Municipal Community' to the business entries, 
     which can be used for joining them with the economic/demographic data of their corresponding Municipal Community
- 3. Joining - Business - Neighborhood Data: adding the feature 'Neighborhood' to the business entries, which can be
     used for joining them with the features of their corresponding community
- 4. Joining Neighborhood - Municipal Community Data: adding the feature 'Municipal Community' to the neighborhood entries
